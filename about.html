OUR STORY

MEET KEVIN HAYES

The Heart of the Diner

The Village Diner is proudly locally owned and operated by Kevin Hayes, a well-known figure in the Johnson City community. Kevin is frequently seen on the floor, personally greeting customers, filling coffee cups, and ensuring the diner always feels like "home." Customers describe him as a friendly, hands-on leader who is often found cleaning windows or helping his staff during the busiest shifts.

THE GROOVY IDENTITY

No Shortcuts. Just Alchemy.

Our leadership leans into a unique 80s and Grateful Dead theme, filling the walls with pictures, tie-dye decor, and "Hey Now" greetings. We maintain a strict set of old-school values, anchored by our "no shortcuts" handcrafted quality approach. Whether it's our homemade corned beef hash or our fresh-ground chuck for burgers, we take pride in making every meal from scratch.

A NEIGHBORHOOD STAPLE

Local Giving & Community First

Under Kevin’s leadership, the diner is known for participating in local charity drives and community events. We aren't just a place to grab a quick bite—we are a central hub for the residents of Johnson City to gather, connect, and thrive.
